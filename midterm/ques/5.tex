Probability to solve hash-puzzle  $ = p$ \\ 
Ways to choose $k$ miners out of $n$ = $ \binom{n}{k}$ \\
For k miners, prob. to solve hash-puzzle simultaneously 
\begin{align}
	=& \binom{n}{k} (p)^k (1-p)^{n-k}
\end{align}
